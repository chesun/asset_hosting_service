Survey Overview

This survey is divided into three parts. Each part begins with its own set of detailed instructions. Below is a brief overview of the tasks you will complete:

  1. Part 1: Make guesses about a group of workers.
  2. Part 2: Receive more information about those workers, then make the same guesses again.
  3. Part 3: Act as an employer to hire workers.

Bonus

You can earn bonus payments in the following ways:

  1. We will ask you to complete two comprehension-check quizzes on the instructions: one before Part 1 and one before Part 3. You will receive $0.50 for each quiz you answer correctly on your first try.
  2. At the end of the experiment, we will randomly select 20% of participants to receive a bonus based on their choices. If you are selected, we will randomly pick one question from your survey responses and use your answer to determine your bonus.
