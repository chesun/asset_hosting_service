Survey Overview

This survey has 3 parts. We will provide detailed instructions in the beginning of each part. As an overview, here's what you will do in each part:

  1. Part 1: make guesses about some workers
  2. Part 2: learn more information about the workers, and make the same guesses again as in Part 1
  3. Part 3: play the role of an employer to hire workers

Bonus

There are a few ways you can earn a bonus:

  1. We will ask you to complete two comprehension check quizzes about the instructions, one for Part 1 and one for Part 3. You will receive $0.5 for each quiz you answer correctly on the first try.
  2. At the end of the experiment, we will randomly select 20% of participants to be paid a bonus based on their choices. If you are selected to be paid a bonus, we will randomly select one question from the survey and use your answer to determine your bonus.
