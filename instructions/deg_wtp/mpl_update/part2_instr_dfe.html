Part 2 Instructions

Part 2 Overview

  • In Part 2, you will be answering the same questions as in Part 1. However, this time you can learn more about the same group of workers by drawing workers at random.

Part 2 Details

  1. In order to help you make more accurate guesses, you can randomly draw workers by clicking a button labeled "Draw a Worker". Each click will randomly select one worker from the group of 100 workers (every worker is equally likely), and show you the score range (1–3, 4–6, 7–9, or 10–12) of that worker. This does not affect your earnings; it simply gives you more information about the group.

    Example:
    In this example, 2 workers were randomly drawn: one who scored between 4 and 6 points, and another scored between 7 and 9 points (highlighted in red).
  2. You can draw a worker every 3 seconds and do it as many times as you like. Each draw is always from the same 100 workers.
  3. Bonus calculations in Part 2 is the same as Part 1. More accurate answers increase your chances of earning a higher bonus.
