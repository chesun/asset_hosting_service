Part 2 Instructions

Part 2 Overview

  • In Part 2, you will be answering the same questions as in Part 1. However, this time you can learn about this group of workers by drawing 20 of them at random.
 

Part 2 Details

  1. In order to help you make more accurate guesses, you can randomly drawn workers by clicking a button labeled "Draw a Worker". Each click will randomly select one worker from the group of workers (every worker is equally likely), and show you the score range (1–3, 4–6, 7–9, or 10–12) of that worker. This does not affect your earnings; it simply gives you more information about the group.
    Example: 

    In this example, 2 workers were randomly drawn: one who scored between 4 and 6 points, and another scored between 7 and 9 points (highlighted in red).
  2. You can draw a worker every 3 seconds. Once a worker has been chosen, they cannot be chosen again (i.e., no worker appears more than once). Continue until you have drawn exactly 20 workers.
  3. Bonus calculations in Part 2 is the same as Part 1. More accurate answers increase your chances of earning a higher bonus.
