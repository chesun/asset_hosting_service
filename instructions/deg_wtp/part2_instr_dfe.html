Part 2 Instructions

Part 2 Overview

  • In Part 2, you will be answering the same questions as in Part 1. However, this time you can learn about the Worker Groups by sampling workers from them.
 

Part 2 Details

  1. In order to help you make more accurate guesses in this part, you can "sample" from this group of workers. We will show you a button like the one below, and each time you click the button, it will randomly draw one worker and show you which interval (1 to 3, 4 to 6, 7 to 9, and 10 to 12) the worker's score is in. This won't affect your earnings -- it's just a chance to learn about workers in each group.
    Example: 

    In the example above, you have clicked to sample a worker and the computer randomly selected a worker who scored between 4 and 6 points (shown in red).
  2. You can sample workers as many times as you like. However, there is a ${e://Field/draw_cooldown_sec} second "cool down" period each time after you sample before you can click the button again. This is to make sure you take the time to understand the information. 
  3. Each time you sample, the computer will put the sampled worker back into the group before you sample again.
  4. The information you learn in Part 2 will be helpful for completing your tasks better in Part 3.
  5. At the end of the experiment, we will randomly select 20% of participants to be paid a bonus based on their choices.
  6. If you are selected to be paid a bonus, we will randomly select one question from the survey and use your answer to determine your bouns. If you are selected to be paid a bonus, and the selected question is in Part 2:
    1. If the question is to guess the number of workers with each possible score:
      One of your answers in this question is randomly selected, and you are paid a 8 EC (Experimental Currency) bonus if your answer is within ±2 of the correct answer. Otherwise you are paid 0.
    2. If the question is to guess the average score/age:
      You are paid a 8 EC bonus if your answer is within ±2 of the correct answer. Otherwise you are paid 0.
