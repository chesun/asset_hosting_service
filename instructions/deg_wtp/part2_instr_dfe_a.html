Part 2 Instructions

Part 2 Overview

  • In Part 2, you will be answering the same questions as in Part 1. However, this time you can learn about this group of workers by drawing workers from them.
 

Part 2 Details

  1. In order to help you make more accurate guesses, you can "draw" some workers from this group. We will show you a button like the one below, and each time you click the button, it will randomly draw one worker (every worker is equally likely to be drawn) and show you which interval (1 to 3, 4 to 6, 7 to 9, and 10 to 12) the worker's score is in. This won't affect your earnings -- it's just a chance to learn about these workers.
    Example: 

    In the example above, you have clicked to sample a worker and the computer randomly selected a worker nicknamed Min who scored between 1 and 3 points (shown in red).
  2. You can draw a worker every 3 seconds and as many times as you like. Each time, the computer draws from the same 100 workers.
  3. Bonus calculations in Part 2 works the same as Part 1. Basically, more accurate answers will increase your chance of earning more bonus.
