Part 2 Instructions

Part 2 Overview

  • In Part 2, you will be answering the same questions as in Part 1. However, this time you can learn more about the same group of workers by drawing workers from them.

Part 2 Details

  1. In order to help you make more accurate guesses, you can "draw" some workers from this group. We will show you a button (example button below), and each time you click it, it will randomly draw one worker (every worker is equally likely) and show you which interval the worker’s score is in (1–3, 4–6, 7–9, or 10–12). This won't affect your earnings; it’s just a way to learn about these workers.
    Example:
    In this example, you clicked to sample a worker and the computer randomly selected a worker nicknamed Min, who scored between 1 and 3 points (shown in red).
  2. You can draw a worker every 3 seconds and as many times as you like. Each time you draw, the computer selects from the same 100 workers.
  3. Bonus calculations in Part 2 work the same way as in Part 1. Essentially, more accurate answers will increase your chance of earning a higher bonus.
