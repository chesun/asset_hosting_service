Survey Overview

This survey has 3 parts. We will provide detailed instructions in the beginning of each part. As an overview, here's what you will do in each part:

  1. Part 1: make guesses about some worker groups
  2. Part 2: learn more information about the worker groups, and make the same guesses again as in Part 1
  3. Part 3: play the role of an employer to hire workers

 

Bonus

In this survey, all bonuses will be paid in Experimental Currency units (EC). Any EC you earn will be exchanged to USD at the end of experiment following the conversion rate of 1 EC = 0.5 USD.

There are a few ways you can earn a bonus:
  1. We will ask you to complete two comprehension check quizzes about the instructions, one for Part 1 and one for Part 3. You will receive 1 EC bonus for each quiz you answer correctly on the first try.
  2. At the end of the experiment, we will randomly select 20% of participants to be paid a bonus based on their choices. If you are selected to be paid a bonus, we will randomly select one question from the survey and use your answer to determine your bonus.
