Part 2 Instructions

Part 2 Overview

  • In Part 2, you will be answering the same questions as in Part 1. However, this time you can learn more about the same group of workers by drawing random workers.

Part 2 Details

  1. In order to help you make more accurate guesses, you can "draw" workers by clicking a button labeled "Draw a Worker" (see below). Each time you click it, one worker will be randomly selected from the group of 100 (every worker is equally likely). You will see which score range (1–3, 4–6, 7–9, or 10–12) that worker falls into. This does not affect your earnings; it simply gives you more information about the group.

    Example:
    In this example, a worker nicknamed Mateo was randomly drawn, whose score was between 7 and 9 points (highlighted in red).
  2. You can draw a worker every 3 seconds and do it as many times as you like. Each draw is always from the same 100 workers.
  3. Bonus calculations in Part 2 is the same as Part 1. More accurate answers increase your chances of earning a higher bonus.
