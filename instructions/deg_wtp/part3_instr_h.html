Part 3 Instructions

 
  1. You will answer a comprehension quiz for the following instructions. You have 2 attempts for each question.
  2. In Part 3, you will play the role of an employer.
  3. You will be presented with 4 workers who are randomly selected (each worker has equal chance of being selected) from our group of workers. You will see a nickname, profile avatar, and a short profile for each worker.
    Example Profile:
     
  4. For each worker, you will make a series of choices between hiring the worker and receiving an amount of money (shown below). You will select which option you prefer in each row. To make it simpler, the left option is pre-selected, and you only need to select where you want to switch from the left to the right. The rows below that will autofill.
    Example:
     
  5. The choices you make do not affect the earnings of the workers. However, they will affect your bonus.
  6. Payment: One of your choices is randomly selected to calculate your bonus.
    1. If you chose the hire the worker (option on the left), you will earn $1 for every point the worker scored. 
    2. If you chose to receive the money (option on the right), you will receive that amount as your bonus.
