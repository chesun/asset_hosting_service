Part 2 Instructions

Part 2 Overview

  • In Part 2, you will be answering the same questions as in Part 1. However, this time you can learn more about the same group of workers by drawing random workers.

Part 2 Details

  1. In order to help you make more accurate guesses, you can "draw" workers by clicking a button labeled "Draw a Worker" (see below for a hypothetical example). Each time you click it, one worker will be randomly selected from the group of 100 (every worker is equally likely). You will see the nickname and score range (1–3, 4–6, 7–9, or 10–12) of that worker. This does not affect your earnings; it simply gives you more information about the group.

    Example:
    In this example, 2 workers were randomly drawn: one nicknamed Emily who scored between 7 and 9 points, and another, Kevin, who scored between 4 and 6 points (highlighted in red).
  2. You can draw a worker every 3 seconds and do it as many times as you like. Each draw is always from the same 100 workers.
  3. Bonus calculations in Part 2 is the same as Part 1. More accurate answers increase your chances of earning a higher bonus.
